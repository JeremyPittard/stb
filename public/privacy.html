PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Information We Collect

Lock 'n Roll uses local storage to save your game progress. This data is stored locally on your device and includes:

  • Game state (which tiles have been shut)
  • Number of rolls used
  • Whether you've used the Burn feature

We do not collect any personal information.

How We Use Your Information

The data stored locally is used solely to:

  • Remember your game progress between sessions
  • Ensure each player gets one daily puzzle

We do not share, sell, or transfer any information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests. For more information about how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any analytics or tracking services beyond advertising.

Children's Privacy

Lock 'n Roll is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
